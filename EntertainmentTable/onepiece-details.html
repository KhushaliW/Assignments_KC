Story:

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy, a young pirate whose body gains the properties of rubber after eating a mysterious Devil Fruit. Along with his Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy sails across the Grand Line in search of the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece." His dream is to become the King of the Pirates while making friends, facing powerful enemies, and exploring the world's greatest mysteries.
